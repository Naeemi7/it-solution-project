  • About Us

  • IT Solution was founded in 2020. It’s stated promise was “To provide world class Information Technology remote support, keeping its customer’s data privacy as it’s top priority, and we are just one click away. Today IT Solution is one of the high-ranking Information Technology companies and a proven reputation for keeping IT Solution’s original promise. The Remote Helpdesk service in IT Solution provides technical support and assistance to customers, on the phone. our primary intention is to ensure a client’s satisfaction and ability to properly operate any machinery or technology they may be having trouble with. We began our journey on 2020 when the Pandemic brought drastic changes to the way companies in all sectors operate. All the companies needed to run their business remotely and most of the employee lost their jobs. We as IT Solution company proactively came to the play to provide innovative and creative remote services to our clients who works from home with the needed skills to maximize effectiveness, efficiency and productivity in their daily work. We at IT Solution gathered a team IT-Professionals across Germany with an objective to foster efficiency, create coherence, and ensure our client’s businesses operate smoothly in the future.
